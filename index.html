Far Flights

farflights.com

Our website allows users to search for flights by the maximum price they are willing to pay rather than the date they want to fly. It then tells them what dates they would be able to fly at that price point Instead of searching for a flight from other sources (which takes some time), our website only searches our own database for such flights which is almost instant.

Our database gets updated every 45 minutes to provide our users with the cheapest and up to date flights available.
